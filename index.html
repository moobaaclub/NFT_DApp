We are happy to introduce

Derivative NFTs art works

Today NFT collections are mainly static and lifeless. dNFT uses Machine Learning to empower collectors and give them an opportunity to become co-creators. Now they can mint NFT derivatives using the original NFT their own.

A collector chooses NFTs, derivation method and parameters. Our dApp generates unique artwork. These derivatives will carry distinctive traits of the originals and could be algorithmically re-created at anytime.

NFT derivates open myriads of exciting opportunities. Collectors can grow their collections by minting unique derivatives. Creators can earn royalty not only from original works, but also from derivatives when they are sold or re-sold.

As a result, we all enjoy a more vibrant NFT econsystem :)

Our first derivative generation method

Neural Style Transfer

This proof-of-concept implements only one derivation method - Neural Style Transfer. Neural Style Transfer attempts to re-draw one picture usig a style of another picture. It runs purely in browser and is implemented with TensorFlow.js
This site may have problems functioning on mobile devices. Don't worry, you can still read the description below!

How to use it?

Choose a content NFT (left) and apply to it the style of a another NFT (right). The resulting image can be minted as a derivative NFT.

The DApp initialy starts in demo mode with two images (Chicago and seaport). To work with your real NFTs, please connect with the Terra Extension for Chrome (Bombay-12 testnet).
